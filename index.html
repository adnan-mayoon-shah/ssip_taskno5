Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah

Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah inspired the World in general and South Asia in particular through his charismatic and visionary leadership. As a statesman, the Quaid led the constitutional struggle that culminated in the creation of an independent homeland for the Muslims of the Sub-Continent. The Freedom Movement is a living testimony of the political insight and sagacity of Quaid-e-Azam who adhered to the ideals of democracy and rule of law. By the grace of Almighty Allah, Pakistan is progressing socially, economically and politically. We have to continue to strive for making our country a place where people live with freedom, dignity and honour, as envisioned by the Quaid. Let us reaffirm on this day that we will devote our energies in individual and collective capacities for the betterment of our State and society. Our people are enlightened and resilient. The best way to pay tribute to the Father of the Nation is to adhere to the principles of Unity, Faith and Discipline, and to carry forward the Quaid's legacy in letter and spirit toward making Pakistan a developed and prosperous country.
